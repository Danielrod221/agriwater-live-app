How It Works

A simple, secure process for buying and selling water allocation.

A Quick Note on Transfers: Our marketplace facilitates the transfer of water allocation credits, not physical water. A successful transfer is only possible if the buyer and seller are in the same groundwater sub-basin.

For Sellers: How to List Water

Step 1: Create Your Listing

First, create your secure account. Once registered, you can post your water for sale or lease by providing details like amount, price, and water district. Your listing is now live.

Progress: ✅ Listed

Step 2: Receive a Purchase Order

When a buyer purchases your allocation, the deal is automatically accepted. You'll be instantly notified that your listing has been sold.

Progress: ✅ Listed → ✅ Purchased

Step 3: Finalize the Paperwork

A formal agreement is generated and sent to both parties via DocuSign. Once signed, our team guides you through the final water district transfer paperwork.

Progress: ✅ Listed → ✅ Purchased → ✅ Paperwork Finalized

For Buyers: How to Secure Water

Step 1: Find a Listing

Click the "Marketplace" tab to browse all available water allocations you are eligible to receive and find one that meets your needs for the irrigation season.

Step 2: Purchase Water Allocation

When you find the right listing, you can purchase it directly. An agreement is then automatically generated and sent to your email for a secure DocuSign signature.

Progress: ✅ Purchased

Step 3: Finalize the Paperwork

After signing the agreement, our team will guide you through the official transfer process with the water district, providing email updates at every stage.

Progress: ✅ Purchased → ✅ Paperwork Finalized
